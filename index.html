Our Team

Our Office Hours are 8:00AM-5:00PM, Monday through Friday. We are closed for lunch 12noon-1:30PM.

If you need to reach us after hours, please call (254)399-6545 and the on-call physician will return your call. If it is an emergency, please dial 911.

We accept most Insurance Health Plans. If you are unsure if we are a participating provider, please call our office. You can also email: info@wacoprimarycare.com

Self-Pay discounts. We offer discounts to patients that do not have insurance. *please note-due to our contracts with insurance plans, we are unable to offer self-pay discounts to insured patients.

Lab Services - our preferred lab is CPL-Clinical Pathology Laboratory, they are available Monday through Friday from 8am-1pm and 2pm-5pm. Please verify your insurance's preferred lab by calling the number on the back of your card and notify us at the time of your visit.

We are located at the corner of Old McGregor Rd and Londonderry.
